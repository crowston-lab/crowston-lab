About Our Research

The goal of our research is to understand why advancing age predisposes to loss of retinal ganglion cells and from this determine new therapeutic approaches for protecting the optic nerve in glaucoma and other optic neuropathies.

Our Scientists

We are pleased to form an international as well as interdisplinary research team to work together for vision recovery strategies. With the collaborative effort, we are aiming to discover the secrets behind aging related eye diseases

  • Dr.Crowston is a Professor at Duke-NUS Medical School, and a glaucoma consultant at the SNEC. He focuses on understanding why ageing predisposes individuals to optic nerve damage in glaucoma and developing new therapeutic approaches to boost neuronal repair.
